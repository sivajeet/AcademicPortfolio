About Me

Sivajeet is a budding researcher preparing to join the Chair of Software and Systems Engineering under Professor Alexander Pretschner at the Technical University of Munich. Currently, he is employed as a Data Acquisition Engineer at Volvo Group, where he extensively explores data domains to derive insights and drive innovation. With a Master's degree in Software Engineering and Technology from Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden and a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Silicon Institute of Technology, India, Sivajeet has enriched his knowledge through experiences at Volvo Cars, Good Solutions, and APTIV. Beyond work, he finds joy in sports like cricket and volleyball, relishing the thrill of competition. An enthusiastic traveler, Sivajeet enjoys discovering new destinations. Check out his Instagram for some travel snapshots.
