About

In July 2024, I started my PhD at the Chair of Software and Systems Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Alexander Pretschner at the Technical University of Munich. My research focuses on exploring the potential of Generative AI, particularly Large Language Models (LLMs), for code migration and modernization.

I hold a master's degree in Software Engineering and Technology from Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden. Prior to starting at the chair, I was employed as a Data Engineer at Volvo Group. Additionally, I have gained experience as a student engineer at Aptiv and Good Solutions.
